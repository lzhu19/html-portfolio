Best Movies According to Laura

A list of movies that left an impression.

Can be seen as the only movies I remember, because it's been age since I've actually seen a movie.

1. Spy x Family

This is the only movie I've watched in the past three months. It was pretty good! It was very well-animated, and the music was really good. I enjoy animated action movies, so this was within my favorite genre.

2. Mission Impossible Part 1

This is the only other movie I've watched recently. It was very loud and hurt my ears, but the music itself was something I appreciated. The brass and woodwinds meshed so well together, and I could feel the intensity of each scene precisely because the music aided with the entire atmosphere. I wish they turned down the speakers though.
